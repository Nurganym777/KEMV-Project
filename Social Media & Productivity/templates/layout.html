About The Project

Understanding the complex relationship between digital habits and workplace effectiveness

Our Methodology

This analytics platform synthesizes data from multiple sources:

  • Digital behavior tracking
  • Productivity self-assessments
  • Workplace health surveys
  • Job satisfaction metrics

Data is collected through anonymous surveys and processed through our analytics engine to identify patterns and generate actionable insights for different organizational levels.

Key Findings

Users who take regular digital breaks show 23% higher productivity scores

Reducing daily notifications by 50% correlates with 15% less reported stress

Optimal social media usage for productivity: 30-45 minutes per day in short bursts
